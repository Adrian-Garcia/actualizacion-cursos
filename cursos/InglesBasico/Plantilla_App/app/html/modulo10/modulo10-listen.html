Listen and Repeat

En esta sección escucharás conversaciones de diferentes personas y situaciones, para que te familiarices con el idioma.
No te preocupes si no entiendes cada una de las palabras, ¡trata de comprender qué es lo que quieren decir!
Si lo deseas, puedes apoyarte con un diccionario para buscar las palabras clave en cada conversación.
¡Adelante, hazlo, tú puedes! Es uno de los ejercicios más recomendados para aprender a expresarte oralmente en el inglés.

I feel sick / Me siento mal

Mrs. Jones: How do you feel?

Dolores: Terrible! I have a cold

Mrs. Jones: Do you have a fever?

Dolores: I’m not sure. Do I feel warm?

Mrs. Jones: Let me check. Oh yes! You are very warm. Let me check your temperature. Yes, you do have a fever 103 F. That's high. You must take some aspirin to help you get your fever down.You need to go home and rest. Hope you feel better soon.

Dolores: Thanks Mrs. Jones. I’m going home now, I feel terrible!


 

Call the doctor / Llama al doctor

Carla: What's wrong. Miguel?

Miguel: I have a headache and my stomach hurts, too! I think I'm going to be sick

Carla: You must get to bed right away. I’ll call the doctor.  Hello. Dr. Hart? This is Carla Rodriguez. My husband, Miguel is very sick

Dr. Hart: What are his symptoms?

Carla: He has a headache, his stomach hurts and he is vomiting

Dr. Hart: Where is he now?

Carla: He is in bed

Dr. Hart: It sounds like the flu. Tell him to stay in bed today. Give him aspirin for the headache. Make sure he has plenty to drink. It’s okay if he does not want to eat. If he is hungry. You can give him plain foods like bananas rice applesauce or toast. If his symptoms do not get better or he does not stop vomiting please call me back right away. It’s important that he has plenty to drink. I hope he feels better and call it his condition does not change

Carla: Thank you Dr. Hart

Dr. Hart: Good-bye



 

To the Bank / Al banco

Mr. Cruz: Hello. I'm sorry to bother you. Could you tell me where the First Star Bank is?
Tomas: Yes. That bank is just around the comer from here. Go to the corner and turn right. Walk straight and the bank is at the end of the block.
Mr. Cruz: Oh, thank you very much.
Tomas: No problem.
