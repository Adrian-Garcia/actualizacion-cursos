Diálogo A

  • Fred: Hello Miguel. This is Virginia.
  • Miguel: Hello Virginia. My name is Miguel.
  • Virginia: It is nice to meet you Miguel.
  • Miguel: Where are you from, Virginia?
  • Virginia: I am from Costa Rica. Where are you from?
  • Miguel: I am from Spain
  • Virginia: How old are you Miguel?
  • Miguel: I am 22 years old- why?
  • Virginia: Because you look younger.
  • Miguel: Thank you, very much.
  • Virginia: You are welcome.
  • Miguel: Would you like to go have some coffee?
  • Virginia: I would love to.

Diálogo B

  • A: How are you?
  • B: I am fine, thanks. How are you?

Diálogo C

  • A: How are you?
  • B: Fine, thanks. And you?
  • A: Fine (Thanks)
