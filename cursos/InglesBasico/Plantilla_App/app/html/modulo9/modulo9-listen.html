Listen and Repeat

En esta sección escucharás conversaciones de diferentes personas y situaciones, para que te familiarices con el idioma.
No te preocupes si no entiendes cada una de las palabras, ¡trata de comprender qué es lo que quieren decir!
Si lo deseas, puedes apoyarte con un diccionario para buscar las palabras clave en cada conversación.
¡Adelante, hazlo, tú puedes! Es uno de los ejercicios más recomendados para aprender a expresarte oralmente en el inglés.

Listening

  • Dialogue A

A man talking to a bank teller / Un hombre hablando con un ejecutivo de cuenta

Bank Teller: Good Morning, sir. Can I help you? 
Luis: Yes, please. I would like to cash this check. 
Bank Teller: All right. Please sign the back of this check. Do you have an account with us? 
Luis: No. I'm sorry. I don't. I'm new here. 
Bank Teller: That's okay. This check is from our bank. You don't need an account here. Do you have and I.D.? 
Luis: I.D.? What is that? I don't understand. 
Bank Teller: I'm sorry. I.D. means identification like a driver’s license or any kind of identification with your photo on it. 
Luis: I have a resident alien card Is that good? 
Bank Teller: Let me check with my supervisor. Yes, that's fine. Don't forget to sign the back of the check.
Luis: Oh, I'm sorry. Here you are. 
Bank Teller: How would you like the money? 
Luis: I don't understand. 
Bank Teller: That means do you want to get your money in big bills like $100's and $50's or do you want smaller bills like $20's, S10's and $5's?
Luis: Smaller bills, please. 
Bank Teller: Here you are, sir: 20, 20 dollar bills, 6, 10 dollar bills, 5, 5 dollar bills and 10 singles. That makes $495.00. 
Luis: That's correct. $495.00. Thank you for all your help. 
Bank Teller: No problem. Have a nice day!


 

To the Laundromat / A la lavandería

Elizabeth: Excuse me. Is there a Laundromat in the neighborhood?
Woman: No, there isn’t. The closest Laundromat is on Main Street.
Elizabeth: Is that far?
Woman: Yes, it is very far. Do you know where Main Street is?
Elizabeth: No, I'm new in this city.  Could you tell me how to get there?
Woman: Drive straight down this street for about two miles. When you see a Target store, turn left. That’s Golf Road. Drive down Golf Road for about five blocks. When you see “Main Street" turn right. The Laundromat is on your right side
Elizabeth: Thanks.
Woman: That's okay. I hope you find it easily Have a good day.

 


To the Bank / Al banco

Mr. Cruz: Hello. I'm sorry to bother you. Could you tell me where the First Star Bank is?
Tomas: Yes. That bank is just around the comer from here. Go to the corner and turn right. Walk straight and the bank is at the end of the block.
Mr. Cruz: Oh, thank you very much.
Tomas: No problem.
