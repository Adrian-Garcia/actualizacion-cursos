Reading

Esta seccion esta disenada para desarrollar tus habilidades de lectura y comprension del idioma ingles. En este momento sera mas facil para ti entender la lectura, ya que se ha utilizado el metodo se comprender el significado del contexto sin utilizar algun metodo de traduccion gramatical. Lee el siguiente material y contesta las preguntas.

Reading Comprehension

  • Calling Grandma
  • Q1
  • Q2
  • Q3
  • Q4
  • Q5

Mom: (Ring, ring.) Hello, mother? This is Elena. How are you?
Grandmother: Hi, Elena. It's nice to hear from you! How are Juan and the children?
Mom: They’re fine. Maria wanted to tell you something-Maria, come to the telephone. It's your grandmother in Mexico.
Granddaughter: Hi, grandma! How are you? I miss you so much! When will you visit us again? Grandma, guess what? My tooth fell out last night. Good-bye grandma, love you!
Grandmother: Oh, my dear Maria. I love you, too.

1. Who is calling who?
  1. Elena is calling her mother.
    Correcto!
  2. Maria is calling Elena.
    Incorrecto!
  3. Elena is calling her daughter.
    Incorrecto!
  4. Maria is calling her grandmother.
    Incorrecto!
2. Where does grandma live?
  1. In France.
    Incorrecto!
  2. In Europe.
    Incorrecto!
  3. In Mexico.
    Correcto!
  4. In China.
    Incorrecto!
3. What happened to Maria?
  1. Nothing.
    Incorrecto!
  2. Her tooth fell out las night.
    Correcto!
  3. She is feeling sick.
    Incorrecto!
  4. Her tooth fell out last year.
    Incorrecto!
4. Is Elena writing a letter to her mother?
  1. Yes
    Incorrecto!
  2. No, she is writing a letter to her father.
    Incorrecto!
  3. No, she is talking with her daughter on the phone.
    Incorrecto!
  4. No, she is talking with her on the phone.
    Correcto!
5. Did the grandma visit Elena and her family before?
  1. Yes, she did.
    Correcto!
  2. Never.
    Incorrecto!
  3. No, she did not.
    Incorrecto!
  4. No, she can’t travel.
    Incorrecto!
6. How can she style your hair?
    li class="choice">
    She can curl hair or straighten hair.
    Correcto!
  1. She can grow hair.
    Incorrecto!
  2. She can burn hair or straighten hair.
    Incorrecto!
7. Is she a good stylist?
  1. Sometimes.
    Incorrecto!
  2. Yes, she is!
    Correcto!
  3. No, she is not.
    Incorrecto!
8. Does she have many costumers?
  1. No, she does not.
    Incorrecto!
  2. Sometimes.
    Incorrecto!
  3. Yes, she has many costumers.
    Correcto!
